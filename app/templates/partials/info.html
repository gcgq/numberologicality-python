Info

This is a Numerology Calculator. Simply enter a name and birthdate and it will calculate:

  • The Destiny number, derived from all of the letters in a person's full name.

    (sometimes called the "Expression Number")

  • The Soul Urge number, derived from the vowels in a person's full name

    (sometimes called the "Heart's Desire Number" or "Inner Soul Number")

  • The Personality number, derived from the consonants of a person's full name

    (also called the "Character Number" or "Expression Number" on some websites)

  • The Life Path number, derived from a person's birthday

    (called the "Destiny Number" in some sources. Seriously, different websites are all over the place with these names)

If you happen to have a 'Y' in your name that acts as a vowel (names like "Mary" or "Tony"), you can place a "!" in front of it, and the app will treat it as a vowel. Actually, you can use it to swap any letter's vowel or consonant status. Go nuts if you want to.
